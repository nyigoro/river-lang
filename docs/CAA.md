# Contributor Assignment Agreement (CAA) v1.0

Effective date: 2026-03-05

Project Steward legal name: Donald Nyigoro

This Contributor Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is between:
- "Contributor": the individual or entity submitting a contribution.
- "Project Steward": the legal entity named above that operates RIVER-LANG.

This file is a project policy template and is not legal advice.

## 1. Assignment of Rights
The Contributor assigns to the Project Steward all right, title, and interest in and to each contribution intentionally submitted to the RIVER-LANG repository, including related copyright and neighboring rights, to the fullest extent permitted by law.

## 2. Patent Rights
To the extent assignment is not effective in a jurisdiction, the Contributor grants the Project Steward an irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, transferable, sublicensable license to any patent claims necessarily infringed by the contribution.

## 3. License Back to Community
The Project Steward may license accepted contributions under Apache License 2.0 (or later project-approved open-source terms). This enables public redistribution while preserving project governance.

## 4. Contributor Representations
The Contributor represents that:
- the contribution is original or properly authorized for submission;
- submission does not knowingly violate third-party rights;
- no confidential or export-restricted material is intentionally included without authorization.

## 5. Moral Rights and Further Assurances
Where allowed by law, the Contributor waives or agrees not to assert moral rights that would block project use of the contribution. The Contributor agrees to execute reasonable additional documents needed to perfect this assignment.

## 6. Acceptance Method (Electronic Signature)
A Contributor is deemed to have accepted this Agreement when both conditions are met in a pull request:
1. The pull request includes the checked attestation line:
   `- [x] I have read and agree to the Contributor Assignment Agreement in docs/CAA.md.`
2. The Contributor has a matching entry in `docs/CAA_SIGNERS.md` with:
   - GitHub username
   - legal name
   - CAA version
   - date (UTC, `YYYY-MM-DD`)
   - evidence link (PR or issue)

## 7. Governing Terms
If a separate signed contributor agreement exists between the Contributor and Project Steward, that agreement controls where terms conflict.
